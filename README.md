
# BSMAI v0.5

BSMAI is a software that works with different assistant to creete Prompt Processes.


### Top features of BSMAI

* **Create pre-build Prompts Processes**
Create pre-built prompt processes and populate data from Google Spreadsheet and Google Docs to automate communication with your Assistant.
* **Your private conversations are not being used for training!**
Gemini, Claude and ChatGPT uses your private conversations history to train its models and if you disable it then you lose all your conversation history!
* **Use Gemini, Claude and GPT-4 without three $20 / month subscriptions, you don't even need a single $20 subscription!** You only pay as much as you use. BSMAI costs nothing so you just pay for your Gemini, GPT-4 and Claude 3 API usage.
* **A very polished interface with great mobile support** You can "install" on your mobile phone by opening your instance of BSMAI in your Safari browser, tapping the Share icon, and then selecting "Add to Home Screen".
* **You will never hit the '*You've reached the current usage cap*' errors**.
